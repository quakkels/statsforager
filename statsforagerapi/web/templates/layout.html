What's Feed Forager all about?

Our vision is supported by three pillars:

  1. Exploration!
  2. Independence!
  3. Discovery!

Exploration

Feed Forager will help you explore the wild internet through Blog feeds. Feed Forager is not a search engine! It doesn't use a traditional search algorithm. Instead, it uses a blog you provide to forage through and find other blogs.

The basic assumption is: blogs you like will link to other blogs you might like. So you can give Feed Forager a starting point, then you can walk through results, pausing to appreciate anything that catches your eye.

Independence

Feed Forager and the underlying RSS Discovery Engine is completely independent of internet gatekeeping giants such as X (Twitter,) Google, Facebook, and Microsoft.

Discovery

We exist so that you can discover creators and content that are niche and new, even if they aren't "gatekeeper approved."
